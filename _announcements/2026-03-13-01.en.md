RDF Portal will be unavailable during the following period due to scheduled maintenance of the NIG supercomputer.

This maintenance is conducted in accordance with the legally mandated power outage at the National Institute of Genetics (NIG), and all related services will be suspended during this time.

===Period
From midday on 13 March to 24:00 on 19 March 2026 (24-hour clock)

We apologize for any inconvenience and appreciate your understanding.
